Hello, I'm Austin Redd

I’m a designer -  I solve problems and tell stories through beautiful and fuctional user interfaces. I am driven by a desire to continuously learn about the ever-changing web and the impact design has on the world.

Before moving to the Denver area in 2011 I lived a number of places including Houston, Texas and Orlando, Florida. While in Orlando I attended Full Sail University where I received a bachelor’s degree in digital arts and design and a master’s degree in entertainment business. After moving to Denver in 2011 I joined Hero Design Studio in downtown Denver for a short 3 month internship. After my departure from Hero Design Studio I joined Room 214 as an interactive designer. I am currently freelancing full time in the Denver and Boulder area.

USAA Salute to inspiration

Every year Intersport does several Facebook applications to showcase different plays, players, and arenas in the college sports world. In 2013 they teamed up with USAA to honor military athletes who inspire us to greatness.

Werthers Original Product Promotion Landing Pages

Horizon Organic Mobile Website

Horizon Organic noticed a large amount of traffic to their site coming from mobile devices. This mobile version of their website was created to give users access to all the same information in a great mobile experience. I was responsible for the UI and UX design and the front end development work.
